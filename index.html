Forward Points (IDR pips above spot)
NDF rate = Spot × (1 + IDR rate × T) / (1 + USD rate × T). Hedge cost = annualised cost of buying USD forward. Higher IDR rates = more expensive hedging for importers.

RM Talking Points
CCS spread = IDR fixed leg − USD SOFR. Corporates with USD bonds can swap to IDR fixed via CCS. CCS IDR fixed ≈ NDF implied − ~40bps liquidity premium.
